Hello user, my website is all about volleyball! This website is about the basics of volleyball, how to play and few locations around Kansas that you can play at! Volleyball is a fast-paced team sport that combines athletic skill, strategy, and teamwork. The objective is to send the ball over the net and hit it on the opposing team’s side of the court while preventing the other team from doing the same.

Types of Moves: Hitting, Setting, Passing, and Blocking

More Information

Volleyball can be played indoors on a court or outdoor on sand! They do have their differences, but are both equally as fun. The sport requires and builds on reflexes, communication, and precise coordination. It's played from all ages and can be casual recreational games or competitive professional leagues.
